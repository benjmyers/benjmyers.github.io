Title IX Investigation Tracker

The Chronicle of Philanthropy

Online Giving Trends

How do donors give throughout the year? This project gives readers a unique look at when donors give and the causes they support. Using Python to run a weekly analysis on online giving data, the text for this article is programmatically generated.

Python and MySQL were used to automate weekly data analysis, along with QGIS and TileMill for rendering map tiles. Driveshaft populates the interactives (D3 and Leaflet) with data.

The Chronicle of Higher Education

The Flagship Diversity Divide

Recent protests at universities nation-wide have raised an important question: just how diverse are colleges? Using data from the Department of Eduction, I analyzed the diversity of faculty and students at flagship universities in all fifty states. Comparisons of student and faculty diversity with that of the region and nation as a whole show the stunning lack of diversity currently found on flagship campuses nationwide.

MySQL was used for data analysis, and visualizations are D3 and D4. I also created the static graphics.
